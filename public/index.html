About

Tubbies is a collection of 10,000 cute NFTs living on the Polygon blockchain, created from a combination of over 200 traits with varying rarity across different categories. There will be special 1/1s in this collection for a few lucky minters! Our vision is to spread positivity and bring more joy into the world. We hope that this collection will bring people together through community, creativity, and fun art!

Roadmap

We will continue to create cute/fun art and work on developing & growing our community.

We have plans to open an online gift shop with different collectibles that will include items like apparel, plushies, stickers, and more! The token holders will get exclusive discounts/access to the various items on the gift shop.

We also have plans on collaborating with various projects & talented artists.

We have many ideas for the future of Tubbies, so join us as we launch our project and expand the Tubbies universe!

FAQ

What is the supply & mint price?
- There will be a total of 10,000 Tubbies available. Mint price 1 MATIC.

When is the launch date?
- We are targeting March, 18 2022 for our launch.

What is the utility?
- Holders of Tubbies will have a fun & cute piece of art, plus access to our close-knit community, holder-exclusive giveaways, contests, priority in future collections, and more!

What are NFTs?
- NFT stands for “non-fungible token” which are unique digital items that are stored and encrypted on the blockchain that people can buy, own, and trade. NFTs can simply be digital art but can have various benefits. They can be treated like membership cards where holders get exclusive access to things like websites, events, merchandise, and more.
